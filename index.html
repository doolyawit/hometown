Top three activities to do at Chiang Mai

See Doi Inthanon

one of the most popular national parks in Thailand. It is famous for its waterfalls, few trails, remote villages, viewpoints, sunrise/sunset watching,

Chiling Thanon Nimmanhaemin

Whilst many tourists visiting Chiang Mai visit the shops of local artisans in the Old City, Nimman is where the younger crowd and students shops at trendy boutiques and shopping malls with entertainment venues.

See Doi Tao Lake

The name Doi Tao Lake probably rings a bell to most people, as there are stories of it. Before you go on a vacation there, surely you must want to know some.
